About Us?

The Amu Language Institute in Herat, as the first educational center, offers high-quality in-person classes and academic counseling. This center provstartides the opportunity to participate in online language exams such as Duolingo and TOEFL, and operates based on the Q: Skills for Success curriculum. Additionally, informational seminars and introductions to international study opportunities help learners. With a modern environment and advanced facilities, Amu has become the first choice for those seeking quality language education.
